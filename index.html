Realization Engine

Turning specifications into realized systems.

The Realization Engine is a body of work investigating how humans and AI systems collaborate to move ideas from concept to working artifact. The projects gathered here share a common thesis: that durable software is built from explicit specifications, validated theory, and architectures that make their own assumptions visible. Each repository contributes a different layer to that program, from the formal frameworks underneath to the tools that put them into practice.
